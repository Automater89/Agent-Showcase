How We Got Here

The Journey

From first agent to enterprise governance — this is how a portfolio of 41 AI agents came to life at WACKER Chemical.

Early 2025
First Agent — Proof of Concept
Built SmartDeck V.1 as an experimental proof that AI could replace manual slide creation. Validated the concept internally and earned buy-in for further development.
Mid 2025
Portfolio Expansion Across Domains
Scaled from 1 to 20+ agents, branching from Documents into IT Helpdesk, HR, Legal, and process automation. Each agent addressed a specific, measurable pain point.
Late 2025
SmartDeck 2.0 & Multi-Agent Architecture
Evolved from single agents to an orchestrated ecosystem — the SmartDeck Router routes requests intelligently across Builder, Converter, and domain-specific agents.
Early 2026
Agent Governance Phase 1 — Enterprise Adoption
Led formal governance rollout ensuring all 41 agents meet compliance, security, and policy standards. This phase formalized AI deployment as an enterprise capability, not just a side project.
